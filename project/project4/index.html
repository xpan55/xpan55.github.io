Lexical Conceptual Representations in Bicultural Bilinguals

Investigated conceptual representations in Chinese-English bicultural bilinguals
bicultural bilingual conceptual representation ERP

This project has been published on Bilingualism: Language and Cognition:

Pan, X., Xiong, A., Jouravlev, O., & Jared, D. (2021). Conceptual representations in bicultural bilinguals: An ERP approach. Bilingualism: Language and Cognition, 24(5), 932–944.

and has been presented at the Psychonomics Society 2015 Annual Meeting, Chicago, USA:

Jared, D., Xiong, A., Pan, X., & Jouravlev, O. (2015, November). Lexical conceptual representations in bicultural bilinguals. The Psychonomics Society 2015 Annual Meeting, Chicago, USA.
